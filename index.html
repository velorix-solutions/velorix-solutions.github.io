Our Story

HOW WE
STARTED

Velorix was founded with a single mission — to make powerful AI automation accessible to businesses of all sizes. We saw the gap between complex AI tools and real-world business needs.

Starting with eBay dropshipping automation for UK and USA markets, we expanded into full-stack AI solutions — chatbots, data pipelines, LangChain agents, and beyond.

Today, Velorix serves clients worldwide, delivering automation systems that save time, reduce costs, and drive measurable growth.
